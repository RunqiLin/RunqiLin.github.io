Biography

I am currently a final-year PhD student at The University of Sydney, supervised by Prof. Tongliang Liu. Before this, I received my Bachelor's degree in Data Science from Beijing Information Science and Technology University in 2021, supervised by Prof. Wei Zhang. I was a visiting student at the University of Oxford, MBZUAI (Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence), and Tsinghua University. My research interests lie in trustworthy machine learning, with a particular emphasis on robust generalization and human alignment within foundation models.
